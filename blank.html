How to use QR codes
1

QR codes need to be placed in large numbers, so that customers have easy access to them. And also in order for customers to understand that receiving feedback is really important to you.

2

Often the staff have a negative attitude towards QR-codes, as customers can easily complain about the work of the personnel. The employees may hide QR codes, unfold the signs with them, do everything so the QR codes will not be noticeable.

3
